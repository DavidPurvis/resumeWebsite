About
My name is David Purvis, and I am Software Engineering Student from Jackson, MS. Every since I was a little boy, I was always obsessed with electronics. I was so perplex how solid state object, liked the GameCube, could produce a NFL game that I can control on to my TV. I knew it was not magic, and something had to have been causing the game to appear on the screen and react to the buttons I was pressing on my controller. It was not until later life, when I actually found how they worked. I became consumed by video games to the point where I wanted to build my own. Upon researching how to build a computer and what parts should I buy, I realized my passion was computers. At the time, however, I thought the parts of the computer were the most fascinating part of the computer because of how powerful they were and what they could do. When senior year of High School rolled around where I took two coding classes when I found my true love was coding. I was able to pick it up fairly quickly and realized I had a talent for it. Eventually during those classes, finishing the assigned program for each class was not enough, so I began to find ways to minimize the number of lines it took me to write the code. This is where I say the beauty of coding. Now, I am in college at Mississippi State studying software engineering just like I have always wanted to. Maybe one day, I will be able to give back to the industry that has entertained me so much.
